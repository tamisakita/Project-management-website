ABOUT US

Who we are

Founded in the vibrant city of Vancouver, Van-Core-Fitness was created by a small group of like-minded fitness enthusiasts who shared the same passion for fitness, health and the driven desire to create a fantastic community that promotes people to challenge themselves and achieve their health/fitness goals no matter how small or big.

We are family, and we welcome everyone with open arms to our friendly- neighborhood gym. So when choosing a gym that is right for you, leave the hard work to your workouts and make the easy choice to be a part of a winning team with us at Van-Core-Fitness!

Vision

Our vision is to be the go-to destination for fitness and wellness, inspiring individuals to lead healthier, happier lives through our exceptional facilities, expert guidance, and vibrant fitness community.

Mission

Van Core Fitness is dedicated to empowering individuals to achieve their fitness goals, fostering a welcoming and supportive community, and promoting lifelong health and well-being.
